<p>Hungary has <a class="key">3,152 municipalities</a> as of July 15, 2013. There are 346 towns and 2,806 villages. Out of the villages, 126 are large villages.</p>

<ul>
  <li>The <a class="key">capital</a>, Budapest, has a special status and is not part of any county.</li>
  <li>25 towns are called cities with county rights.</li>
</ul>

<p>Four cities (Budapest, Miskolc, Győr, and Pécs) have <a class="key">agglomerations</a>. There are also 17 other areas in development.</p>

<p>The largest city is Budapest, and the smallest town is Pálháza with 1,038 people (2010). The largest village is Solymár with 10,123 people (2010). Over 100 villages have fewer than 100 people, with the smallest having fewer than 20.</p>
