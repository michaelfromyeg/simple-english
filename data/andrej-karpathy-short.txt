<p><a class="key">Andrej Karpathy</a> is a <a class="key">Slovak-Canadian computer scientist</a> born on 23 October 1986. He works in <a class="key">artificial intelligence</a> and <a class="key">deep learning</a>.</p>

<p>Andrej was born in Bratislava, Slovakia. At age 15, he moved to Toronto with his family. He earned his bachelor's degrees in <a class="key">Computer Science</a> and Physics from the University of Toronto in 2009. In 2011, he completed his master's degree at the University of British Columbia.</p>

<p>Andrej received his PhD from Stanford University in 2015. His studies focused on combining natural language processing and computer vision.</p>

<p>He created the first <a class="key">deep learning</a> course at Stanford. It became very popular, growing to 750 students by 2017.</p>

<p>Andrej was a founding member of <a class="key">OpenAI</a>. He worked there from 2015 to 2017. In June 2017, he joined <a class="key">Tesla</a> as the director of artificial intelligence. He worked under Elon Musk. He left Tesla in July 2022.</p>

<p>In February 2023, he returned to OpenAI. Later, in February 2024, an OpenAI spokesperson confirmed Andrej had left the company again.</p>
