<p><a class="key">Earth</a> is the <a class="key">third planet from the Sun</a> and the only one known to have life. It has <a class="key">liquid surface water</a> in its <a class="key">ocean</a> that covers most of the planet. The rest is land with plants, ice caps, and <a class="key">tectonic plates</a> that move and form mountains, volcanoes, and earthquakes.</p>

<p>Earth has a <a class="key">dynamic atmosphere</a>. It has mostly nitrogen and oxygen and forms clouds. Greenhouse gases like carbon dioxide trap heat from the Sun, keeping water liquid and maintaining an average surface temperature of about 15°C (59°F).</p>

<p>Earth is <a class="key">round</a> like a ball with a circumference of about 40,000 km. It is the largest rocky planet. Earth orbits the Sun in about 365 days and rotates every 24 hours, causing day and night.</p>

<p>Earth has a <a class="key">moon</a> that stabilizes its tilt, causing seasons. Earth formed 4.5 billion years ago and had life that changed the atmosphere and surface. Humans appeared 300,000 years ago and now impact the climate and environment significantly.</p>
